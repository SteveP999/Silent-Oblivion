Silent Oblivion

Silent Oblivion moves through sound the way a ghost moves through a room — felt before they're seen. Their music lives at the intersection of indie pop and alternative, where dreamy textures meet hooks that refuse to leave.

Their debut album Beginnings Past is a collection of singles built around bold ideas and bigger feelings — from the breezy momentum of Cruisin to the sun-soaked energy of Surfin to the hauntingly catchy Lip Gloss Ghost.

Released through Hello Texas Records. Already in motion. Just getting started.
